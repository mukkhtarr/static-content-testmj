  • Trade Sample

    Last month Metrc introduced a new feature in CCTT to licensees, making it easier to designate trade samples in the system. Distributors, retailers, and microbusiness licensees can now identify test-passed trade sample packages by adding a Trade Sample flag. For more information about this feature, please review the Metrc Bulletin.
