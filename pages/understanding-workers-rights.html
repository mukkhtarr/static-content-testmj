As a Cannabis business owner, it’s important to have a general understanding of the laws and best practices related to your role as an employer. The resources on this page outline some areas you should be aware of when it comes to worker’s rights.

Understanding labor peace agreements

  • Labor peace agreements for cannabis businesses

    State law requires many commercial cannabis licensees to enter into, and abide by, labor peace agreements. Recent changes to state law have strengthened these requirements, including by expanding the number of licensees that are subject to these requirements. This document is intended to help educate cannabis licensees and workers about state-law requirements related to labor peace agreements […]
