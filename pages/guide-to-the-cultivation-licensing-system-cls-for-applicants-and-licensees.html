Introduction

The Department of Cannabis Control (DCC) oversees two separate cannabis licensing systems. This is a guide to the Cultivation Licensing System (CLS). In this document, we will show you how to register with the licensing system, set up your account, and apply for a cannabis cultivation license. In addition, this guide will include how to upload and attach the documents you must provide with your application, and how to pay application and license fees.

Other helpful resources are available on the DCC’s application resources page or by clicking here. Including reference documents with more details on acquiring and maintaining your cannabis cultivation license.

Important Note Regarding Email Addresses

Each person is uniquely identified by an email address and name in the licensing system.
Enter this information carefully and accurately. Errors will greatly slow your application.

  • One person, one email address – do not use multiple email addresses for the same person.
  • Name fields are case sensitive – enter upper and lower case consistently and avoid using all caps or all lower-case.
  • When creating an account, there are two places to enter your email address – be sure it is the same email address for both entries.
  • All Owners must have separate email addresses – one person, one email address, even for partners, married couples, or people in the same household.
  • Do not use parentheses or other special characters in name fields – apostrophes, hyphens, and periods are acceptable to use. Disable your browser’s pop-up blocker – pop-up dialogs are used often in the system.
  • Do not use the Backspace key or the Back function to avoid losing work.
  • Use the Tab key to move from one field to the next – it also jumps to other objects such as Help and calendar functions.
