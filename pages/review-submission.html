Intro to this section

Review

Next is the Review section where you may make edits to previous sections of your Science Amendment.
Select the Edit button to make changes. You will be redirected to the section selected. Once you click Continue, you will return to the Review page.

At the bottom of the Review screen, select the checkbox indicating that you agree to the above certification statement.

Select Continue Application to submit your Science Amendment.

Submission

The next screen is a notice that your Science Amendment was submitted.

Note the Science Amendment number at the bottom.

If there is any missing information in your Science Amendment, the Department of Cannabis Control (DCC) Licensing staff will send you a deficiency notice, which you will receive via email.

If you would like to check the progress of your Science Amendment’s review process, select the Science Amendment record number (CCLYY-#######-SA##) in your Licenses list, select the Record Info dropdown list, and choose Processing Status.

Note: If/When your Science Amendment is approved, you will receive an email from DCC with instructions on how to view your Science Amendment record and, if necessary, how to print your new license certificate.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your Science Amendment, you may contact DCC by sending an email to licensing@cannabis.ca.gov or by calling 1‑844‑61‑CA‑DCC (1‑844‑612‑2322).
