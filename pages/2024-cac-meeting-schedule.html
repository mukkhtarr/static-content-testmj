The Cannabis Advisory Committee (CAC) will convene quarterly, on the second Thursday of the applicable month, as follows:

  • March 7, 2024
  • June 6, 2024

The current membership of the CAC will end their two-year terms in August 2024. Dates for the fall and winter quarters will be decided by the new slate of members during their first official meeting.

Dates are subject to change. Public notice of meeting dates will be provided at least 10 days in advance of the scheduled meeting.

Subcommittee Meetings

Subcommittee meetings will be scheduled ad-hoc as requested by the subcommittee chair in collaboration with DCC. Dates are subject to change. Public notice of meeting dates will be provided at least 10 days in advance of the scheduled meeting.
