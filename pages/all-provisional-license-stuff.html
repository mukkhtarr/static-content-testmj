  • Requirements for Provisional Licenses

    Assembly Bill 141 and Senate Bill 160 (2021) modified requirements for provisional licenses. Licensees must now meet additional criteria to renew a provisional license to ensure progress is being made toward annual licensure. Provisional licenses will sunset in stages over time.
