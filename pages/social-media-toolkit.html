Thank you for helping to raise awareness about Real CA Cannabis! In this toolkit you will find two clips from our PSA video as well as two static social media posts to share on your channels. Our goal is to spread the word about Real CA Cannabis statewide and empower cannabis consumers to use our search tool to quickly and easily find and verify licensed dispensaries near them.

How to use this toolkit

Each post below is designed to be shared on Facebook, Instagram, X, and/or TikTok. Here is how to post using this resource: 

  1. Copy and paste the post copy onto the social media platform. Please make sure to remove any portions of the post copy that are not applicable to the platform. For example: Remove references to a “link in bio” on all platforms other than Instagram.
  2. Download the image or video you plan to post and upload to the platform. You can find all
    assets here.
  3. Tag our account in the image and/or post copy, and include our campaign hashtag
  4. Link to the Real CA Cannabis website in the post copy (or in your account bio if posting on
    Instagram)
  5. Share!

We recommend waiting a few days between each post so the content stretches further. Likewise, try to avoid sharing both PSA video clips back to back.
